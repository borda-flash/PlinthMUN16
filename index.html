About Us

PLINTH, LNMIIT brings you LNMIIT MUN'16 in association with United Nations Information Centre for India And Bhutan (UNIC), in its third edition at its campus, The LNM Institute of Information Technology on 23rd-24th January,2016".

LNMIIT Model United Nations Conference is organised with the motive of experiencing the intellects sharing their knowledge diplomatically of the currently prevailing worldly issues related to policy making, security & defence, human rights & welfare , culture and relations and many more in the form of extempore , debating and discussing, the kind that happens in the various councils of UNITED NATIONS.

We seek to employ within our participants a variety of communication and critical thinking skills and diplomatic competence in order to represent the policies of their country. We strive to channelize your grey cells to resolve the intricacies pertaining to the global matters and help you evolve as an eminent leader through dialogues and discussions. With our ceaseless efforts, we wish to make a healthy debating environment where the delegates could think out of the box and can generate some sound innovative ideas through peaceful means that will be beneficial to all the society.

We at LNMIIT, ensure you to provide a holistic environment and the best of comforts along with perfectly planned councils and agendas and not to forget, an accomplished and experienced Executive Board and make it one of the best experiences of your MUN journey.

Organiser's Desk

Dear friends, It gives me immense pleasure to bring you the 2nd edition of LNMIIT Model United Nations in collaboration with United Nations Information Centre for India and Bhutan (UNIC), to be held on 23rd-24th of January, 2016 at LNMIIT, Jaipur. It is my privilege to lead the team which will be your host for this conference. Every MUN that I have been a part of, has strengthened the belief, that an MUN is the most complete form of debate. It is a great opportunity to enhance skills in research, writing, public speaking, problem solving, consensus building and crisis management. It gives you the honor of being an ambassador of a country. More importantly, it entrusts you with the task of solving global issues. With LNMIIT MUN, we intend to provide you a platform to discuss and deliberate on realistic issues through an assemblage of committees, led by a brilliant Executive Board. The team is committed to make your experience enjoyable and rewarding through a structured environment. We hope to make the conference a landmark MUN and aspire for your support in doing so. I welcome you again and look forward to see you all here at LNMIIT MUN 2015.
